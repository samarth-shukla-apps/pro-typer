Pro Typer ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Pro Typer, available on the Google Play Store.

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details. However, we may collect the following non-personal information automatically:

The app requests the following permissions:

No other sensitive permissions are required or used.

We use Google AdMob to show ads. These ads may be personalized based on your usage or generalized based on region and preferences. You can opt out of personalized ads in your Google account settings.

6. User Accounts

The app does not require or support user registration or login. You can use all features without creating an account.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties except for analytics and advertising as mentioned above. No data is sold or misused.

8. Children’s Privacy

Pro Typer is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Your Rights

Since no personal information is collected, there’s no user-identifiable data to access, modify, or delete.
